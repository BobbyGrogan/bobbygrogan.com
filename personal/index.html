Lichess
I started playing chess a lot my senior year of high school. I joined the chess team and competed with the team at the State competition. I would guess my peak actual ELO is 1300, but I have no FIDE ranking. My highest chess.com ELO was 1550 in rapid. Lichess ELOs are definitely inflated, so I don't think my skill is reflected there.
