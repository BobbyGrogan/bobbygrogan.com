Global Commodity Market Research

Description: This research project is about the global commodity market. Commodities are phyiscal or primary goods, and the market is where they are bought and sold in bulk volume. I divided the market into three categories: Metals+, Energy, and Grown. In my presentation and paper I elaborate on what products are traded, what countries are signficant, and how these goods are traded.
